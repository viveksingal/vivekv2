Date: November 1st - 28th 2015

Online: brainstation.io

Tools: Adobe Photoshop | Sketch | Trello | Wordpress | Nikon d750 |

1. Case study

After completing front-end and back-end development BrainStation approached our Cohort and requested us to build a brand new student portal with the help of Educators and members of Konrad Group.

2. Design Problem

The Design problem that was presented to us “ Students and educators had very basic access to what could be displayed and added to the portal & the user flows, and user journey’s where not structured to be an optimal digital solution”

3. Objectives

  • Progress tracking ( Visibility of system status)
  • Live searching content ( User control and freedom)
  • Consistency and standards of the BrainStation brand
  • Aesthetic and minimalist design
  • Improving uploading systems & Flexibility

    • 4. Process

    As the project was be worked on there were many phases of user & client testing. During these tests new problems and ideas were brought up and implemented before the next sprint review. At the end of the two weeks we had designed and developed a web application for BrainStation that is being used by current students.
